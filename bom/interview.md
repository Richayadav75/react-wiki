# BOM Interview Questions

1. **What is the difference between window.location and window.history?**
   - `location` is used to get/set the current URL. `history` allows navigating backward and forward through the browser's session history.

2. **What are cookies vs. LocalStorage vs. SessionStorage?**
   - Cookies are sent to the server with every request. LocalStorage persists until deleted. SessionStorage persists only for the current tab session.

3. **What is the purpose of the navigator object?**
   - To get information about the browser (name, version, language) and the user's device (geolocation, battery status).
