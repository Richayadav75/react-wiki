# Loops Interview Questions

1. **What is the difference between `for...in` and `for...of`?**
   - `for...in` iterates over the enumerable **keys** (indices) of an object or array.
   - `for...of` iterates over the **values** of an iterable object (like an array or string).

2. **How can you break out of a loop?**
   - Use the `break` statement to exit the loop entirely, or `continue` to skip the current iteration.

3. **What is an infinite loop and how do you avoid it?**
   - A loop that never meets its exit condition. Avoid it by ensuring the condition eventually becomes false (e.g., incrementing a counter correctly).
